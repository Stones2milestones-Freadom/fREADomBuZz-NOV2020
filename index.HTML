Clouded leopard: the best climber among cats

 

Did you know that clouded leopards are wildcats with marks on their bodies that look like clouds? This is why they are called clouded leopards! They are the best climbers in the cat family and can hang upside down on trees.

 
 

Coriander: a common ingredient in Indian food

 

The coriander plant has got its name from the Greek word ‘koris’, meaning insect. Also called cilantro, the plant is cultivated in many parts of the world for its use in cooking. Although the entire plant is edible, the leaves and seeds are the parts most often used in cooking.

Building the core language skills

As parents and guardians are often a child’s first ‘teacher’, we have curated this ‘ready reckoner’ exclusively for you to help you guide your child on the path of language learning. Keep an eye on this space for more insights, tips, and how-to’s! 

There are four pillars of language learning: listening, speaking, reading, and writing. These are also known as the core language skills. 


Speaking is the ability to verbally express our thoughts in a way that is clear and meaningful.

 

Listening is the ability to understand what has been spoken.


Writing is the ability to express our thoughts in a way that is clear and meaningful through the written word.

 

Reading is the ability to understand what has been written.

Explore our Activities section on the app for fun and interactive activities to help your child develop these skills!
